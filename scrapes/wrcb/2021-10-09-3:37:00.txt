Updated: October 9, 2021 3:37 PM

The rest of your Saturday will feature a mix of sun and clouds as temperatures
fall back into the 70s this evening. Skies will remain clear tonight with lows
ranging from 58-63 degrees. Patchy dense fog will develop across much of the
area after 3 AM.

Areas of dense fog will be around Sunday morning with generally sunny skies
during the afternoon hours. Temperatures will remain warm, topping out in the
80s.

Monday through Thursday will feature a very similar pattern. Highs will reach
the 80s each afternoon with overnight lows dropping into the 60s. A stray shower
will be possible on Tuesday, but the rest of the upcoming week will remain dry.

Rain chances may return late Friday into Saturday as a potential cold front
approaches the area. Fall temperatures could also be in store for the Tennessee
Valley. Stay tuned!
