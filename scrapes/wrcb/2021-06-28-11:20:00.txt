Updated: June 28, 2021 11:20 PM

Danny is now onshore as a tropical depression. The 11pm advisory had it with 35
mph winds and only scattered showers and storms. As the remnants of the storm
move to our south we will have more scattered storms mainly Tuesday afternoon
and Tuesday evening. Highs will reach the upper 80s in the valley, low 80s in
the mountains.

Wednesday and Thursday will be hot and muggy with a few passing showers or
storms each afternoon.

A strong front will move through Friday bringing widespread rain and storms
Friday morning through Friday night. Some of the showers and storms could linger
into Saturday. Highs Friday and Saturday will only reach the low 80s. Sunday
looks to be dry.
