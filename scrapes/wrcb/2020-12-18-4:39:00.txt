Updated: December 18, 2020 4:39 AM

Good Friday! It's a bitterly cold start this morning with temps in the low to
mid 20s. We will be mostly sunny this afternoon and a little warmer than
yesterday with highs in the upper 40s.

Saturday temps will range wildly from 27 in the morning to 52 in the afternoon
with sunny skies.

Overnight Saturday into Sunday morning we will see rain showers with temps
dropping into the mid to upper 30s. We may get a light wintry mix on the
plateau. Through the day rain showers will continue on and off. Most areas will
get 1/4" to 1/2". Highs Sunday will reach about 50.

We will be mild and dry through Wednesday. Light rain showers will move in late
Wednesday night into early Christmas Eve morning with maybe a light wintry mix
in the mountains. It will be cold Thursday with highs only in the mid 40s.

Christmas Day: We will start cold in the low to mid 20s with some light snow
showers possible early in the morning. Skies will be clearing through the day
with highs in the upper 30s, around 40 in Chattanooga.
