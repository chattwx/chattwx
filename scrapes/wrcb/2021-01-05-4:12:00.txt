Good Tuesday. Cloudy skies this morning are leading to more mild temps in the
30s and 40s. The clouds will clear as we warm only to 51 this afternoon. We will
be MUCH colder Wednesday morning in the mid to upper 20s (30 in Chattanooga). It
will be cool and dry in the afternoon with a high of 50.

We will start cold on Thursday in the low 30s. Low pressure will move through
during the day bringing rain showers in during the late afternoon through the
night. Overnight the rain showers will change over into a wintry mix primarily
in the higher elevations. That will last through the afternoon, ending Friday
evening. Highs Friday will be in the low 40s in the valley, 30s in the
mountains.

Rainfall amounts Thu-Fri will be .5" - 1" in the valley. Less than .25" in the
mountains. Snowfall will be limited to the higher elevations. We could see 1"-2"
falling on Friday.

The weekend will be cold and dry. Lows will be in the upper 20s and low 30s.
Highs will be in the mid to upper 40s.
