Happy Monday, everyone! 

It's a beautiful day across the area with plenty of sunshine, low humidity, and
comfortable temps. Look for highs to climb into the upper 60s to lower 70s.

into the 40s.

Tuesday will feature mostly sunny skies with highs back into the low to mid 70s.

A cold front arrives on Thursday, bringing our next chance for rain to the area
during the afternoon hours. 

Behind this front, another blast of cold air is on the way with highs dropping
back into the lower 50s for the weekend, with lows in the 20s & 30s!
