Updated: June 7, 2021 1:55 PM

The moisture from the Gulf of Mexico will continue to flow into the Tennessee
Valley for much of this week and into the weekend. Summertime temperatures will
prevail with occasional showers and storms. Highs for Tuesday and Wednesday will
be in the low 80's with high humidity. Scattered showers with an isolated storm
will be possible.

Thursday and Friday will see occasional showers and storms with highs in the mid
80's and high humidity. On Saturday and Sunday, the showers and storms will be
more scattered and highs will be in the upper 80's. Next Monday should be dry
with highs near 90.
