Updated: July 4, 2021 10:22 PM

Summertime heat and humidity return to the Tennessee Valley Monday and Tuesday
as daytime temperatures range from 89-94 degrees. Rain chances will be low
through Tuesday, but a stray shower will be possible during the evening hours.

The chance of afternoon showers and storms will be back in the forecast
Wednesday through the upcoming weekend. Temperatures will remain seasonable with
highs in the upper 80s and overnight lows around 70 degrees.

Tropical Storm Elsa will bring heavy rain and wind to Cuba and Jamaica tonight.
Elsa will continue to the north and impact the Florida Peninsula beginning
Monday morning. Tropical Storm Elsa will not pose much of a threat to the
Tennessee Valley.
