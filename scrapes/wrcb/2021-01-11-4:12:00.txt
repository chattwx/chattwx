Updated: January 11, 2021 4:12 AM

Good Monday. We can expect a light wintry mix this morning. Some patchy areas of
ice may be possible in some of the higher elevations, particularly on the
Cumberland Plateau. Temps this morning are in the 30s. We may see a few isolated
rain showers this afternoon with a light wintry mix in the Blue Ridge lasting
into the evening hours. Accumulations will be light. Rainfall will be less than
1/4". Snowfall accumulations will be less than an inch on the plateau. Temps
this afternoon will only make it to the low 40s.

Tuesday through Thursday will feature cold mornings in the upper 20s and low
30s. Highs will be a bit milder in the low 50s.

Friday a weak front will move through bringing clouds and a stray sprinkle or
two.

The weekend will be cloudy and cold with lows in the upper 20s and low 30s and
highs in the 40s.
