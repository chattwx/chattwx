Clouds will be increasing tonight and lows will be much warmer as they approach
50. Skies will be cloudy for Saturday and scattered showers will move across the
Tennessee Valley. Amounts will be light. Highs should be near 60. On Sunday we
will see mostly cloudy to partly cloudy skies with highs in the low to mid 60's.
By Sunday night showers will return with the rain continuing into the first half
of Monday. Rain amounts will be near a half inch.

Tuesday should be partly cloudy and mild with highs in the mid 50's. More light
rain showers will occur on Wednesday and some flurries could get mixed in
especially in the higher elevations. Highs will be near 50. Thursday and Friday
will be mostly sunny with highs in the low to mid 50's. Paul Barys
