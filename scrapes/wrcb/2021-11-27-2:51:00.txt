Updated: November 27, 2021 2:51 PM

Happy Saturday! Skies will remain mostly sunny with a few high clouds streaming
across the region this evening. Temperatures will gradually fall back into the
40s after the sunsets. Tonight, lows will range from the mid to upper-30s under
mostly cloudy skies.

Sunday will be partly cloudy to mostly sunny with highs topping out in the mid
to upper-50s. A dry, reinforcing cold front will pass through late Sunday,
dropping temperatures back below freezing Sunday night. Chilly temperatures will
carry over into Monday as highs range from 48-52 degrees.

A warming trend will begin Tuesday as highs return to the 60s. We will remain
dry Wednesday through Friday with above-average temperatures reaching the mid to
upper-60s each afternoon.
