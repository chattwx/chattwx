Updated: August 1, 2021 1:07 AM

A Severe Thunderstorm Watch is in effect until 4 AM EDT for Marion, Sequatchie,
Grundy, Bledsoe, Rhea, Meigs, Hamilton, Bradley, Dade, Walker, Whitfield,
Murray, Dekalb, and Jackson Counties. 

A fast moving line of storms will be pushing across the area over the next
several hours before weakening by daybreak. Damaging wind gusts of 60+ mph will
be the primary threat. Heavy downpours and frequent lightning will also
accompany these storms.
