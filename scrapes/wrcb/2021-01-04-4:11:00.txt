Good Monday. We are off to a cold and frosty start with temps in the upper 20s
and low 30s. We will warm nicely this afternoon, though. Clouds will build in
and we will make it to a high of 57. This evening will be cloudy with a few
spotty sprinkles possible.

Tuesday will start cloudy and not as cool in the upper 30s and low 40s. We may
even get a sprinkle or two in the Blue Ridge in the morning. Tuesday afternoon
will clear out with highs in the low 50s.

Wednesday will be chilly and dry all day with temps ranging from 31 in the
morning to 51 in the afternoon.

Thursday will be chilly also with temps in the low 30s in the morning and only
climbing to 47 in the afternoon. Expect some late rain showers heading into
Thursday evening.

Friday through the weekend will be dry and cold with lows in the 30s and highs
only in the mid to upper 40s.
