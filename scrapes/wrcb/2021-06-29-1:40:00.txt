Updated: June 29, 2021 1:40 PM

We may see a few isolated showers or storms on Wednesday, with the best chance
of them developing on the plateau and into the Blue Ridge mountains. Highs
should be near 90. The chances for afternoon showers and storms will be
increasing on Thursday with highs in the upper 80's. The chances for
thunderstorms and rain showers will be excellent on Friday with some heavy
rainfall amounts possible. Highs on Friday will be near 80.

This weekend looks dry with cooler temperatures and highs in the low to mid
80's. Monday and Tuesday will remain dry with highs in the upper 80's to near
90.
