Good morning, today will begin with cool temperatures, running a few degrees
colder in most spots than yesterday morning. A Frost Advisory is in effect for
Cherokee and Fannin Counties until 8 and 9am respectively. Patchy frost could
kill sensitive outdoor vegetation. Additionally, a few areas of river valley fog
are developing. The morning time until about 9am is the only period with weather
issues today, otherwise it will be a gorgeous fall day. Sunny skies, a light
north to northeast breeze, and highs in the upper 60s to low 70s.

Tonight will once again be clear and cool with lows in the low to mid-40s.
Tuesday will be mostly sunny with highs in the low 70s. Wednesday will have a
mix of sun and clouds and a bit warmer in the mid-70s. Thursday will be the
active weather day of the work-week with scattered showers and storms. Highs in
the low 70s. Friday will be partly sunny with clouds gradually clearing and
highs near 72.
