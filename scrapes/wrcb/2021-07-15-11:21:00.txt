We wrap up the week with more heat and humidity. The high Friday will reach 91
with the heat index topping out at about 95. We will see some clouds overhead,
and some spotty showers and storms in the afternoon. Your Friday night and
Saturday morning will be rain free.

The weekend we will have highs in the mid to upper 80s with better chances for
scattered storms each day mainly in the afternoon. The humidity will remain high
through the weekend.

Next week we will be in the mid-80s with a few afternoon showers and storms at
least through Wednesday.
