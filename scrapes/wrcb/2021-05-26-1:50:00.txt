Moisture levels will start to rise over the next few days. Humidity levels will
start to rise, but the temperatures may cool down just a bit, too. Highs on
Thursday will be near 90 with some clouds and a small chance for a shower mainly
late in the day.

The chances for any meaningful rain will grow into Friday afternoon and evening
as showers and a few storms will move in. Highs Friday will be in the mid to
upper 80's.

This weekend should be pleasant with highs in the mid 70's Saturday and Sunday.
There will be some clouds on Saturday with a few more breaks on Sunday.

Monday through Wednesday will be dry with highs near 80.
